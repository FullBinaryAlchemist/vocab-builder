Instructions

Study#

You can start learning words by clicking on study link in the navbar or on the sidebar and can view your progress on dashboard

Learn#

You will get thousands of words to learn of GRE & SAT category. You can choose any one of them and start learning. You will get 5 sets of words to learn every time you learn and you need to learn atleast 5 words to go to next 5 words.

Review#

In these you can review words which you studied before. You need to study atleast 5 words to review. As you study atleast 5 words you will be directed to review those words and the words studied before. Words will appear after certain intervals to review and if you get the correct answer for the respective word 3 times then that word will be added to your progress. You can continue to learn as well.
